01 · Context

The Problem

🚨 Real Risk

Information is fragmented

Bank details, insurance policies, wills, and nominee records sit in scattered places — impossible to retrieve quickly in a crisis.

⏱ Costly Delays

Claims & estate settlement slow down

Missing nominees or will details can delay pay‑outs by months and add significant legal costs for loved ones.

🌐 Blind Spots

Cross‑border complexity

Families with UK & India exposure face conflicting jurisdictions, tax residency rules, and repatriation restrictions.
