The Problem

Real Risk

Information is fragmented

Bank details, insurance policies, wills, and nominee records sit in different places and are hard to retrieve quickly.

Costly Delays

Claims and estate settlement slow down

Missing nominees or will details can delay pay-outs and increase legal burden for loved ones.

Blind Spots

Cross-border complexity

Families with UK and India exposure struggle with visibility, compliance context, and jurisdiction-specific planning.

Our Solution

Family Vault turns scattered family-finance records into a structured, actionable readiness system.

360° Financial Inventory

Track UK assets, India assets, bank accounts, investments, insurance, property, and liabilities.

Readiness Intelligence

Family Readiness Score (0–100) and insight engine highlight missing wills, nominee gaps, and low emergency reserves.

Emergency Cover Workflow

Emergency Mode provides a concise view for trusted contacts in urgent situations.

How Emergency Cover Works

Step 1

Preparation

  • Record key assets and contacts
  • Add nominees and will/executor details
  • Upload critical documents
Step 2

Monitoring

  • Insights flag critical gaps
  • Readiness score tracks progress
  • Emergency fund strength is visible
Step 3

Crisis Access

  • Trusted person opens Emergency Mode
  • Gets priority contacts and account locations
  • Acts faster with lower confusion
